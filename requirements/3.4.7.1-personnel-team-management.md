# 3.4.7.1 Personnel and Team Management — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.4.7.1  
**Purpose:** Personnel files, teams, skill matrix for scheduling match.

---

## Screens

### Personnel list (`/mes/personnel`)

| Employee ID | Name | Department | Post | Team | Skill level | Hourly rate |
|-------------|------|------------|------|------|-------------|-------------|

---

### Personnel detail

- Operable processes (multi-select from routing/process library)
- Entry date, contact (mock HR fields)
- Link to wage history

---

### Team management (`/mes/personnel/teams`)

| Team | Workshop | Leader | Members count | Processes / lines |
|------|----------|--------|---------------|-------------------|

Team form: name, workshop, leader, member multi-select, responsible processes/lines.

---

### Skill matrix (`/mes/personnel/skills`)

**Grid:** rows = personnel, columns = processes, cells = skill level (0–4 color scale).

Edit cell inline; used by scheduler for match hints (3.4.3).

**Export** matrix Excel mock.
