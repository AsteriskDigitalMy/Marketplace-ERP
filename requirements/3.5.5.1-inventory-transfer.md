---

## PDA Flow (`/wms/pda/transfers/:id`)

### Stock-out phase

1. Scan source location
2. Scan material + batch
3. Confirm qty

### Stock-in phase

1. Scan destination location
2. Scan same material + batch
3. Confirm qty

**Mismatch guard:** qty out must equal qty in — alert on variance.
