# 3.3.5 Subcontracting and Collaborative Manufacturing — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.3.5

---

## Screens

### Subcontract demand (`/scm/subcontracting`)

Auto from routing — process, product, qty, subcontractor, delivery, processing fee.

---

### Subcontract order

Approval → **material issue** to WMS (scan issue, mock) → track issue/return/consumption.

---

### Receiving & inspection

Arrival inspection: pass → warehouse; fail → rework return.

---

### Settlement

Processing fee reconciliation, invoice → **SAP AP** sync.

---

### Tracking board

| Order | Progress | Material issued | Received | Payment | Overdue |

Alerts on overdue completion.

---
