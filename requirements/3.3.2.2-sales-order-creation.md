# 3.3.2.2 Sales Order Creation and Management — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.3.2.2

---

## Screens

### Order list (`/scm/orders`)

| Order # | Customer | Style | Qty | Amount | Delivery | Status |

---

### Create order (`/scm/orders/new`)

**Source options:**

- **From quotation** — picker loads PDM quotation (mock) → auto-fill lines
- **Manual entry**

#### Header mandatory fields

Customer, order #, delivery date, delivery location, payment method, transport mode, quality requirements.

#### Line items table

Style/model, qty, unit price, amount (calc), batch edit, import CSV (mock).

**Validations before submit:** product exists, price rules, credit check, mandatory fields.

Attachments: contracts, drawings.

Actions: Save draft, Submit for review.

---

### Order detail

Tabs: Lines, Attachments, Review status, Tracking, Changes, Shipment.

Edit locked after effective unless change request.

---
