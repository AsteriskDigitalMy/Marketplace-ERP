# 3.4.3 Workshop Planning and Production Line Scheduling — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.4.3  
**Purpose:** Decompose SCM weekly plans into daily line/team schedules; real-time push to floor; completion tracking.

---

## Screens

### Scheduling board (`/mes/scheduling`)

**Views:** Week | Day | Line Gantt (MVP: day table).

#### Weekly plan import (from SCM)

Panel: SCM workshop plan rows → `Decompose to daily` action.

---

### Daily scheduler (`/mes/scheduling/daily`)

**Grid:** rows = production lines, columns = date or time slots.

Drag work orders onto cells:

- Validates line capacity (mock bar)
- Team skill match indicator (from skill matrix)
- Equipment status warning if fault (link 3.4.6.2)

**Adjust sequencing** — reorder within line; optimize button (mock suggestion).

---

### Team task view (Pad `/mes/pad/schedule`)

Today's tasks for logged-in team:

| WO # | Product | Qty | Line | Process | Due |
|------|---------|-----|------|---------|-----|

---

### Workshop display (`/mes/display/schedule`)

Full-screen daily schedule per workshop — auto-refresh mock.

---

### Completion tracking

| Line | Planned qty | Completed | % | On-time |
|------|-------------|-----------|---|---------|

**Overdue alert** — row highlight + notify workshop director (mock alert in admin).
