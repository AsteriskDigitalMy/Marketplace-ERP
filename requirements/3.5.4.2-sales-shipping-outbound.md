### Shipping notice detail (`/wms/outbound/shipping/:id`)

**Sections:**

- Customer + delivery address
- Finished goods lines
- Auto-generated pick list with route
- OQC status per line — **only Passed may ship**
- Logistics panel (carrier, plate, tracking #, dispatch time)

---

## PDA Flow (`/wms/pda/outbound/shipping`)

1. Select shipping notice
2. Follow pick path (same pattern as production picking)
3. Scan pick + review
4. **OQC validation** before confirm — block if not Passed
5. Optional: vehicle loading scan screen — record carrier, license plate, tracking number
