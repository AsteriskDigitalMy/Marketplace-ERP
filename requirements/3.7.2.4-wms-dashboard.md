# 3.7.2.4 WMS Warehouse Dashboard — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.7.2 — Operational Management — WMS  
**Route:** `/bi/operations/wms`

---

## KPIs

Total inventory value, inventory accuracy, dead stock rate (>180 days), capacity utilization, in/out efficiency, turnover days.

---

## Filters

Warehouse, zone, material attributes, inventory age, consuming department.

---

## Charts

| Chart | Type |
|-------|------|
| Inventory value structure | Pie / donut |
| Capacity utilization trend | Step area |
| Stock age warning | Matrix blocks (age buckets × warehouse) |

Matrix cell click → material list `Sheet`.
