# 3.4.4.4 Full-Process WIP RFID Traceability — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.4.4.4  
**Purpose:** RFID chain from raw material → cut piece → finished product; forward/backward trace.

---

## Screens

### Trace hub (`/mes/traceability`)

Dual search (like WMS 3.5.7):

| Mode | Input |
|------|-------|
| Forward | Raw material batch / RFID |
| Backward | Finished product barcode / RFID |

---

### Forward trace timeline

1. Raw material inbound RFID — batch, supplier, inspection
2. Cutting bundle RFID — material batch link
3. Process steps — RFID reader events, operator, equipment, inspection
4. Finished product RFID — link to cut piece + material chain

---

### Backward trace

From FG RFID → all processes, personnel, equipment, materials, timestamps.

Cross-links to WMS traceability views (mock).

---

### RFID chain diagram

Node graph or nested timeline — click node for `Sheet` detail.

**Export** PDF/Excel for compliance (mock).

---
