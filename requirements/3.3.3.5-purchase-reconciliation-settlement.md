## Screens

### Reconciliation (`/scm/procurement/reconciliation`)

Auto-match: receipts vs PO vs invoices.

| Supplier | PO | Received | Invoiced | Variance | Status |

Online reconciliation view with supplier portal sync (mock).

---

### Invoices

Entry, certification, verification → **SAP AP voucher** sync badge.

---

### Payment plans (`/scm/procurement/payments`)

By account period: payment application, approval, completion updates SAP.

---

### Procurement execution report

Amounts: procured, warehoused, invoiced, paid, outstanding — chart + table.
