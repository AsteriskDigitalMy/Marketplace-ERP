# 3.3.3.2 Procurement Demand and Requisition Management — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.3.3.2

---

## Screens

### MRP demand pool (`/scm/procurement/demands`)

**Sources:** order material, safety stock, wastage, R&D sampling, other.

Table: material, gross demand, available, in-transit, safety stock, **net requirement**, required date, linked order.

`Run MRP` button (mock calculation).

---

### Consolidation

Select multiple demands (same supplier + material) → `Merge to requisition`.

---

### Purchase requisition (`/scm/procurement/requisitions`)

Create from demands or manual. Department + financial approval workflow.

Approved → route to PO creation queue.
