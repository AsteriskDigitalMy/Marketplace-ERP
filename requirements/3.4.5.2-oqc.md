# 3.4.5.2 Outgoing Quality Control (OQC) — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.4.5.2  
**Purpose:** Final inspection before warehousing/shipment; SCM sync; ship gate.

---

## Screens

### OQC queue (`/mes/quality/oqc`)

Auto-generated on FG completion — batch, qty, product, standards, customer requirements.

---

### Inspection form (Pad `/mes/pad/quality/oqc`)

Customer/product grade may change checklist template (mock profiles).

| Result | Next action UI |
|--------|----------------|
| Qualified | Generate warehousing receipt CTA |
| Unqualified | Rework/scrap order CTA |

---

### OQC detail (admin)

Inspection results, inspector, photos, linked WO and sales order.

**SCM sync status** — order quality inspection status updated (mock).

---

### Shipment validation hook

On SCM/WMS ship screens (cross-link): banner if OQC ≠ qualified — **block ship** (mock gate component reusable).

---
