## Process timeline (`/sap/costing`)

| Step | Trigger | SAP CO processing |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| Material issue | WMS pick confirm | Material doc + direct materials cost |
| Production receipt | WMS FG receipt | FG inventory + credit production cost |
| Labor collection | WO operation complete | Direct labor to cost center |
| Overhead allocation | Month-end | Overhead to production costs |
| WO settlement | WO closure | Variance calc + settle to inventory |

### Month-end cost close

**Wizard:**

1. Validate all WO settlements synced
2. Run overhead allocation (mock)
3. SAP completes actual cost accounting
4. **Results inbound** — SAP sends back updated standard costs + variances to ops (mock table)

### Cost analysis (`/sap/costing/analysis`)

Dimensions: product, work order, workshop.

Compare operational totals vs SAP CO totals — variance table + chart.
