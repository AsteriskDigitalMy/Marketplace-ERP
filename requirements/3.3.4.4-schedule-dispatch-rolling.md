# 3.3.4.4 Schedule Dispatch and Rolling Updates — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.3.4.4

---

## Screens

### Plan approval & dispatch (`/scm/scheduling/plans/:id`)

On approve:

- Decompose to **MES work orders** (mock list preview)
- Generate **material plans** + WMS picking lists (mock push status)

Dispatch confirmation dialog with downstream impact summary.

---

### Rolling schedule (`/scm/scheduling/rolling`)

Daily/weekly refresh controls.

**Triggers display:** actual production progress, material arrivals, order changes, equipment breakdowns (mock feed).

`Apply rolling update` → diff view before/after.

**Notifications** — departments notified of schedule changes (mock inbox).

---
