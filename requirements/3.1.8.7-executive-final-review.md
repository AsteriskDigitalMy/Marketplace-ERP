# 3.1.8.7 Executive Final Performance Review — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** F-PERF-007 — Section 3.1.8 Performance Appraisal & Scoring  
**Route:** `/pms/appraisal/final`

---

## Functional Summary

Corporate executive (CFO) completes final approval of employee performance. On approval, results publish network-wide; rejection returns to Auditor for re-verification.

**Input:** Employee ID, final approval opinions.  
**Prerequisites:** Status "Pending final review"; highest final review permission.

---

## Final Review Queue (`/pms/appraisal/final`)

Table: Employee, Department, Grade (confirmed), Score, Routed from (direct A/B vs post-HR path), Status, Actions.

Filters: department, grade, routing source.

Bulk approve (mock) with confirmation — only for rows without open disputes.

CFO sees C/D HR-path employees as **read-only / not actionable** per [3.1.8.4](./3.1.8.4-preliminary-review-shunting.md) (grayed rows with tooltip).

---

## Review Detail (`/pms/appraisal/final/:employeeId`)

### Full-process record (accordion or tabs)

1. Auto scoring & indicators ([3.1.8.2](./3.1.8.2-automatic-scoring-grading.md))
2. Preliminary review & shunting ([3.1.8.4](./3.1.8.4-preliminary-review-shunting.md))
3. HR assistance (if applicable) ([3.1.8.5](./3.1.8.5-hr-rectification.md))
4. Secondary confirmation (if applicable) ([3.1.8.6](./3.1.8.6-secondary-confirmation.md))

### Executive decision

| Field | Required |
|-------|----------|
| Decision | Approve / Reject |
| Final approval opinion | Yes on reject; optional on approve |

**Approve** → status `Published`; employee personal center updated (mock banner).  
**Reject** → status `Returned to auditor`; Auditor preliminary queue flagged for adjustment.
