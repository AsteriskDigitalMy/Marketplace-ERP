**Source:** Section 3.3.1 — Supply Chain Management (SCM)  
**Module role:** Business drive core — sales order as source; closed-loop across sales, procurement, intelligent scheduling, supplier collaboration, subcontracting.

### Customer portal (`/portal/orders`) — read-only mock

Customers view own order progress (3.3.2.5).

---

## Dashboard (`/scm`)

KPIs: open orders, pending reviews, MRP shortages, overdue POs, schedule delays, on-time delivery %.

Pipeline: Order → Review → Plan → Produce → Ship → Close.

Cross-module badges: MES, WMS, PDM, SAP (mock).

---

## Shared types (`src/types/scm/`)

`Customer`, `SalesOrder`, `OrderReview`, `PurchaseRequisition`, `PurchaseOrder`, `SchedulePlan`, `SubcontractOrder`.
