# 3.4.2.3 Work Order Dispatch and Material Picking — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.4.2.3  
**Purpose:** Dispatch approved WOs to workshop; auto picking list to WMS; returns/replenishment.

---

## Admin Screens

### Dispatch board (`/mes/work-orders/dispatch`)

Approved WOs ready to dispatch.

| WO # | Workshop | Line | Pick list status | WMS push |
|------|----------|------|------------------|----------|

**Dispatch** → pushes to Pad + workshop display (mock toast).

---

### WO detail — Materials tab

- BOM-based picking list (generated on dispatch)
- WMS status: Pending / Picking / Complete
- Pick log: picker, qty, time (from WMS mock)
- **Returns / replenishment** sub-section:
  - `Request return` / `Request replenishment` forms
  - Approval → push to WMS (mock)
  - Consumption summary: actual usage, loss rate %

---

## Pad (`/mes/pad/picking`)

1. Scan work order barcode
2. BOM pick list with scan confirmation per material
3. Record picker, qty, timestamp (mock)

---

## Workshop display (`/mes/display/work-orders`)

Large cards: WO #, product, qty, process requirements link, BOM summary — for dispatched WOs per workshop.

---
