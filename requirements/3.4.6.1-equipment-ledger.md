# 3.4.6.1 Equipment Ledger Management — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.4.6.1  
**Purpose:** Equipment master data, documents, status lifecycle.

---

## Screens

### Equipment list (`/mes/equipment`)

| Code | Name | Model | Workshop/line | Status | Last maintenance |
|------|------|-------|---------------|--------|------------------|

**Filters:** status, workshop, line.

---

### Equipment detail (`/mes/equipment/:id`)

**Tabs:** Overview, Documents, Maintenance history, Parameters, Status log.

#### Overview fields

Code, name, model, specs, brand, purchase date, supplier, workshop, line, status, spare parts list, operating procedures summary.

#### Documents

Upload/view drawings, manuals, maintenance handbooks — Pad-accessible preview.

#### Status log

Chronological status changes with operator and reason.

**Status values:** Running, Standby, Fault, Under maintenance, Suspended, Scrapped.

---

### Pad equipment lookup (`/mes/pad/equipment/:code`)

Read-only quick view for operators — SOP, parameters, status.

---
