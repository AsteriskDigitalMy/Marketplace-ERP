# 3.3.4.1 Demand and Resource Management — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.3.4.1

---

## Screens

### Demand aggregation (`/scm/scheduling/demands`)

Consolidate: effective sales orders, forecast orders, stock replenishment.

| Product | Qty | Delivery | Priority | Customer tier |

Priority rules config panel (drag reorder).

---

### Capacity modeling (`/scm/scheduling/capacity`)

Maintain baselines:

| Resource | Capacity |
|----------|----------|
| Factory / workshop / line | Hours per shift |
| Equipment | Availability |
| Shifts | Calendar |

Exclude: maintenance windows, holidays (calendar UI).

Product/process: std hours, equipment, skill requirements (links PDM/MES).

---

### Material kitting (`/scm/scheduling/kitting`)

Per order:

| Material | Required | On hand | In transit | Short | Kitting % |

**Kitting rate** progress bar per order.

Lock scheduling toggle: exclude incomplete-kit orders from schedule board.

Expected arrival dates from open POs.
