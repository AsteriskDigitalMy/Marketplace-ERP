# 3.3.2.1 Customer and Master Data Management — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.3.2.1

---

## Screens

### Customer list (`/scm/customers`)

| Code | Name | Industry | Credit limit | Used credit | Tier | Status |

**Filters:** industry, tier, credit status.

---

### Customer form (`/scm/customers/:id`)

#### Mandatory fields

Customer code, name, industry, contact person, contact info, address, credit limit, payment terms, delivery requirements, inspection standards.

#### Pricing tab

Per-product unit prices and discounts table — customer + product matrix.

#### Documents tab

Qualifications, agreements, historical orders (mock upload/list).

---

### Credit check (order creation hook)

On order save: if `order amount + used credit > limit` → **block submit** with `Alert` and link to customer credit detail.

---
