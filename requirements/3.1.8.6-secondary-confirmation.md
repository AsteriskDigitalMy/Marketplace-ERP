# 3.1.8.6 Secondary Confirmation of Performance Rectification Effectiveness — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** F-PERF-006 — Section 3.1.8 Performance Appraisal & Scoring  
**Route:** `/pms/appraisal/secondary`

---

## Functional Summary

Auditor reviews HR assistance and rectification effectiveness. Pass → route to CFO final review; fail → return to HR for re-rectification.

**Input:** Employee ID, final opinion of secondary review.  
**Prerequisites:** Employee status is "HR Processed".

---

## Secondary Review Queue (`/pms/appraisal/secondary`)

Table: Employee, Department, Grade, HR submitted at, Status, Actions.

Badge: count of items awaiting secondary confirmation.

---

## Review Detail (`/pms/appraisal/secondary/:employeeId`)

### Read-only panels

1. Original appraisal summary (score, grade, indicators)
2. HR assistance records ([3.1.8.5](./3.1.8.5-hr-rectification.md)) — full timeline
3. Linked PDCA proposal status if applicable ([3.1.9.x](./3.1.9.1-pdca-proposal-submission.md))

### Auditor decision

| Field | Required |
|-------|----------|
| Secondary review result | Pass / Fail |
| Final opinion | Yes |

**Pass** → employee status `Pending final review` (CFO queue — note: C/D may still require CFO visibility per policy; mock routes all passed secondary reviews to final queue or marks HR path complete per grade rules).

**Fail** → return to HR with opinion; status `Pending HR Processing`; HR queue repopulated.

Confirmation dialog before submit.

---

## Workflow Diagram (inline help)

Preliminary (C/D) → HR assistance → Secondary confirm ─┬─ Pass → (workflow complete for HR path / optional CFO)
                                                        └─ Fail → HR re-rectification
