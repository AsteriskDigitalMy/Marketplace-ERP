# 3.4.4.1 Cutting Process Management — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.4.4.1  
**Purpose:** Cutting tasks, fabric bundling, piece ID tags, IPQC, outsourcing.

---

## Screens

### Cutting task queue (`/mes/cutting`)

Tasks from routing — product, fabric, specs, layers, sets, delivery date.

---

### Task detail (`/mes/cutting/:taskId`)

#### Fabric calculation panel

| BOM required | Picked actual | Variance | Loss rate % |
|--------------|---------------|----------|-------------|

Compare BOM fabric vs WMS picked qty (mock).

#### Actual cut entry

Record actual cut quantity; auto loss rate.

#### Bundle tagging

After cut complete: generate bundle barcode/RFID per bundle.

**Tag payload display:** WO #, style, size, ply count, piece #.

Print label preview (mock).

---

### Cutting IPQC (`/mes/pad/cutting/inspect`)

Scan bundle tag → dimension/defect checklist → pass/fail.

Fail → rework or scrap routing (link rework module).

---

### Outsourcing (`/mes/cutting/outsourcing`)

Create outsourcing order → PDA outbound scan (mock WMS) → track progress, return, inspection.

---
