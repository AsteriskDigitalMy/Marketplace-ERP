# 3.4.2.2 Work Order Splitting and Merging — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** Section 3.4.2.2  
**Purpose:** Split large WOs by batch/team/line; merge same-product same-process WOs.

---

## Screens

### Split wizard (`/mes/work-orders/:id/split`)

**Step 1 — Source WO summary** (read-only)

**Step 2 — Split strategy**

| Option | UI |
|--------|-----|
| By batch | N sub-orders with qty per batch |
| By team | Assign team per sub-order |
| By production line | Assign line per sub-order |
| By date | Stagger planned dates |

**Step 3 — Sub-work orders table**

Editable rows: sub-WO # (auto), qty (sum must equal parent), team, line, dates.

**Step 4 — Approval preview** → submit for approval.

**Parent link:** each sub-WO shows `Parent: WO-xxx` on detail.

---

### Merge wizard (`/mes/work-orders/merge`)

**Step 1 — Select candidates**

Filter: same product, same process stage, same delivery date.

Multi-select from eligible open WOs.

**Step 2 — Master WO**

Auto-suggest master #; retain original WO details in expandable `Original orders` panel.

**Step 3 — Unified schedule** — single team/line/date for merged WO.

**Approval required** before material plan update.

---

### Operation log (`Sheet` on WO detail)

| Timestamp | Action | Operator | Before | After |
|-----------|--------|----------|--------|-------|

Split/merge entries permanent.
