# 3.1.1.2 Organizational Unit Editing and Disabling — Frontend Requirements

**Source:** F-BASE-002 — Section 3.1.1 System Basic Management  
**Route:** `/pms/admin/org/:id/edit`

---

## Functional Summary

Modify organization information and logically disable idle units without physical deletion. Disabled units and all descendants are hidden from business selectors.

**Prerequisites:** Org management permission; no active approval workflows on unit (mock gate).

---

## Edit Page

Auto-filled form (same fields as [3.1.1.1](./3.1.1.1-organizational-unit-creation.md)).

| Action | Behavior |
|--------|----------|
| Save | Validate sibling name uniqueness; toast + version history entry |
| Disable | Double-confirm dialog: "Unit will be excluded from statistics" |
| Enable | Restore unit (if parent active) |

**Disable with children:** strong alert — "All sub-organizations will be hidden synchronously."

Disabled nodes: grayed in tree, strikethrough label, excluded from org pickers app-wide (mock).
